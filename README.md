<h1>Flixpedia</h1>
    <p>Flixpedia is a web application designed to provide users with comprehensive information on movies, TV shows, and cast & crew. Users can explore detailed content, save their favorite movies and series, and seamlessly manage their watchlist.</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Landing Page</strong>: A clean and intuitive landing page that welcomes users to Flixpedia.</li>
        <li><strong>User Authentication</strong>:
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Sign Up</strong>: Users can create an account to access personalized features.</li>
                <li><strong>Sign In</strong>: Users can sign in to their account to manage their watchlist.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Dashboard</strong>: A personalized dashboard that allows users to access their saved content and recent activities.</li>
        <li><strong>Content Search</strong>:
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Movies</strong>: Search for movies using keywords, and get detailed information.</li>
                <li><strong>TV Shows</strong>: Search for TV shows with an option to view detailed information.</li>
                <li><strong>Cast & Crew</strong>: Explore detailed profiles of cast and crew members.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Content Details</strong>:
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Movie Details</strong>: Users can view detailed information about movies, including the poster, synopsis, and more.</li>
                <li><strong>TV Show Details</strong>: Detailed information about TV shows, including the poster and episodes.</li>
                <li><strong>Cast & Crew Details</strong>: Detailed profiles for cast and crew members.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Saved Content</strong>:
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Saved Movies</strong>: Users can save their favorite movies and view them in their personalized list.</li>
                <li><strong>Saved Series</strong>: Users can save their favorite TV series and view them in their personalized list.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Manage Watchlist</strong>:
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Add to Watchlist</strong>: Save movies or TV shows to your watchlist for easy access later.</li>
                <li><strong>Delete from Watchlist</strong>: Remove unwanted items from your watchlist.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Responsive Design</strong>: Flixpedia is fully responsive, providing an optimal viewing experience on various devices.</li>
    </ul>
    
   <h2 class="tech-stack">Tech Stack</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Frontend</strong>: React.js, Tailwind CSS</li>
        <li><strong>Backend</strong>: Node.js, Express.js</li>
        <li><strong>Database</strong>: MongoDB</li>
        <li><strong>Version Control</strong>: Git, GitHub</li>
    </ul>
